MEGA PROJECT

A symposium to encourage students to make projects that have the capability to change something.

The event was made open for students across all batches and branches. Students worked on a project in groups of at most three and then presented it in the symposium- the final project display round. The projects were from various domains like programming, robotics, graphics designing, etc. The best projects were given certificates to appreciate their efforts and encourage them.
